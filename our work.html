Our Work

Committed to fighting child malnutrition through impactful programs and community engagement

Overview of Core Programs

Our NGO focuses on comprehensive programs designed to prevent, treat, and sustainably reduce child malnutrition. These programs combine nutrition education, healthcare support, community empowerment, and advocacy to create lasting change.

Program Categories

Nutrition Prevention

Community workshops on breastfeeding, balanced diets, hygiene, and kitchen gardening to prevent malnutrition before it starts.

Treatment & Care

Therapeutic feeding, medical care, and micronutrient supplementation for children suffering from moderate to severe malnutrition.

Capacity Building

Training local health workers and volunteers to sustain nutrition programs and empower families with knowledge and skills.

Advocacy & Policy

Engaging policymakers and stakeholders to improve nutrition policies and resource allocation for vulnerable communities.

Project Highlights & Success Stories

Hamdi Ali’s Recovery

Hamdi was found severely malnourished in an IDP camp. With therapeutic food and medical care, she regained strength quickly. Community volunteers supported her grandmother to maintain her health.

Ravi’s Transformation

Ravi, a frail toddler, gained weight and vitality after joining our Nutri-Kitchen program. His mother now actively educates other families on nutrition and hygiene.

Meera’s Garden of Life

Meera and women in her village learned to grow nutrient-rich vegetables, improving diets and generating income through sustainable gardening.

Fatima’s Fight Against Illness

Fatima overcame severe malnutrition and illness with supplements and hygiene education. Her recovery inspired her community to adopt healthier practices.
