HOPE IN EVERY MEAL

HOPE IN EVERY MEAL Foundation

HOPE IN EVERY MEAL has uplifted the health status of children by liberating them from the vicious cycle of illiteracy and malnutrition. The NGO is dedicated to providing nutritious and hygienic mid-day meals to children prepared in the hi-tech kitchen. The meals nourish the body and improve nutritional status among young children. HOPE IN EVERY MEAL's mid day meal program is being implemented in municipal, government aided, and non-aided schools for the benefit of underprivileged children. The work is done without any commercial motive to support the needy students at large without any discrimination on the grounds of religion, caste, creed, or gender. Since the launch of the school lunch program, HOPE IN EVERY MEAL has set up state-of-the-art kitchens in 8 states and 21 cities namely; Tardeo, Mahula, Mira Bhayandar, Nigdi, Palghar, Aurangabad, Wada, Nagpur, Jalna, Delhi, Jamshedpur, Faridabad, Gurgaon, Kurukshetra, Palwal, Kolkata, Jaipur, Kadapa, Nellore, Rajamundary, Ranganarra Gadda, and Tirupati.

Why Malnutrition Matters

Malnutrition is one of the leading causes of death and disability worldwide. According to the World Health Organization (WHO), nearly one in three people globally suffer from some form of malnutrition. In particular, malnutrition is responsible for the deaths of millions of children under the age of five each year. The consequences of malnutrition are devastating, including:

  • Stunted Growth: Children who suffer from malnutrition are at risk of poor physical and cognitive development, which affects their future success in life.
  • Weakened Immune Systems: Malnutrition weakens the immune system, making individuals more susceptible to infections and disease.
  • Increased Risk of Chronic Diseases: Long-term malnutrition can lead to chronic health issues such as diabetes, heart disease, and obesity.
